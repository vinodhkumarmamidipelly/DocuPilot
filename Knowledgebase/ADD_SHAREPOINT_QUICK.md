# Quick Guide: Add SharePoint Site to Microsoft Search

## ⚡ Fast Track (3 Steps)

### **Step 1: Go to Admin Center**
https://admin.microsoft.com/Adminportal/Home#/MicrosoftSearch

### **Step 2: Add Your Site**
1. Click **"Content sources"** → **"SharePoint"**
2. Click **"Add site"** (or "Add content source")
3. Enter: `https://onblick.sharepoint.com/sites/DocEnricher-PoC`
4. Click **"Save"**

### **Step 3: Wait & Test**
- Wait 24-48 hours for indexing
- Test in Teams: Ask Copilot "Show me documents in DocEnricher-PoC site"
